Acts 10-12
Peter's Vision
Peter recieves a vision that tells him to do this...

"Get up, Peter. Kill and eat."

Peter says "Surely not, Lord! I have never eaten anything impure or unclean." But the voice continued:

"Do not call anything impure that God has made clean."

This happened 3 times, and the sheet was taken back.
Then, Cornelius, a gentile, approaches Peter's house. Cornelius represented the "unclean" object. Peter knew to respect him now.
 
The Holy Spirit later descends upon Cornelius.
Peter's Jailbreak
A while later, King Herod decided to arrest and persecute the Church. He arrested many people, including Peter.
One day, however, Peter was able to be rescued from prison by a God-sent angel.
A confused Herod ordered that all of the guards be killed for their apparent "mistake." The king was soon struck down by God afterwards.
