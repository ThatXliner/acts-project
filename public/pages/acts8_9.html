Saul's Journey
This is Saul. He persecuted Christians for a living, and was one of the people who consented to Stephen's death.
Once, as he was walking, Jesus came to him with a blinding flash and blinded him for his cruel behavior. For 3 days he was blind.
Jesus then leads Saul to Annanias, who heals him, and Saul converts to Christianity and becomes one of the major people in the Church. Saul is renamed to Paul.
